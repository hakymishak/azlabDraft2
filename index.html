About Us

Az Lab Sdn Bhd (1346318-H) started from a humble background in 2016 believing in the utmost dedication and integrity in delivering its promises to our partners in any business challenges.

We are a private entity with GMP certification, providing OEM in skincare products services while complying with all NRPA guidelines. We covered more than 100 companies throughout Peninsular Malaysia and our HQ is situated in Muar, Johor. Over the years we have perfected our business models and R&D practices. Today, with the advancements in technology, we are able to deliver better-quality products to you. We also in collaboration with USA, Japan, Korea & Switzerland lab.
